About Me

I am Endy Henry. A specialized teachers' trainer in every form of Montessori education. I am a seasoned educationist in the Montessori, EYFS, Cambridge and Nigeria educational curricular.

I am a Bachelor of Science in Education (B.SC.ED.) graduate from the Rivers State University of Science and Technology (RSUST), Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. I have been an educator for the past decade; I have worked with children of varying academic levels including children with learning difficulties. I have been a teachers trainer for over 6 years. I have trained over 2,000 teachers, helped set up schools with best practices in Rivers and Bayelsa States.

Sharing my learning journey, skills and experiences with teachers and children is my happy place. I love what I do, and I do what I love - Teaching.

I collaborate with Dyslexia Orientation Project (DOP), a non governmental agency (NGO) as a volunteer to help children in Government schools at the Local and State levels, to create awareness and train teachers to sustain the learning of the dyslexic children in their care. I am also one of the cordinators of Teachers and Loving It. A platform created to assist teachers in different sectors and levels to keep abreast with the modern and best practices in the 21st century classrooms.
